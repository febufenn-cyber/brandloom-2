1. Show the Brandloom workspace and approved Phase 3 content.
2. Open Publishing and connect the reviewer account through Meta OAuth.
3. Confirm the returned Instagram username and show that publishing is disabled before confirmation.
4. Confirm the destination account.

7. Show the scheduler state transitions and remote processing status.
8. Open the verified permalink.
9. Edit the original content and demonstrate that the scheduled snapshot remains unchanged.
10. Demonstrate emergency pause and account disconnect.
